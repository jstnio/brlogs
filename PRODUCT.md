# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small to mid-size importers and exporters shipping internationally. They are busy operators — procurement leads, logistics coordinators, business owners — who need a freight partner they can trust without a steep onboarding curve. They evaluate credibility fast: first impression determines whether they reach out.

## Product Purpose

BRLOGS is a freight forwarding company. The website exists to generate business leads and establish authority with SMB importers/exporters. Success = a prospect feels confident enough to make contact. The coming-soon page is the first impression; it must earn a follow.

## Brand Personality

Bold, Global, Decisive. The tone is confident without arrogance — a partner who knows the trade lanes and gets cargo there. No jargon overload. No softening language.

## Anti-references

- Generic logistics: stock-photo trucks, forgettable corporate blue, clip-art containers.
- Startup-pastel SaaS: purple gradients, rounded pill buttons, bubbly tech-startup feel.
- Heavy corporate portal: dense, outdated, government-feeling, form-heavy.

## Design Principles

1. **Authority at first glance.** The page must read as serious and global within two seconds. No hedging visuals.
2. **Confidence over cleverness.** Bold type, clear hierarchy. The brand does not explain itself; it presents itself.
3. **Motion earns its place.** Subtle kinetic elements reinforce "things in motion" — cargo always moving — but never distract from the message.
4. **SMB-approachable, not SMB-cheap.** Premium feel accessible to a mid-size operator, not intimidating enterprise IT.
5. **Brazil identity is an asset.** BR in the name is not a limitation — it is a differentiator for a corridor specialist.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced-motion alternative for all animations. High-contrast text on dark background required.
